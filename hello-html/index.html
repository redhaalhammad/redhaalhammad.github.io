Did you drink enough water today?
Water is everywhere. It is an essential component of life itself. It is in the air we breathe and in the food we eat. As such, ensuring that we keep ourselves hydrated is one of the most important pillars of a healthy lifestyle. Our joints, skin, organs, muscles, and immune system all benefit from proper hydration. Drinking enough water also notably improves mental health and helps regulate serotonin levels. Despite this, many of us tend to neglect or simply forget the importance of staying properly hydrated. To learn more, refer to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention website.
