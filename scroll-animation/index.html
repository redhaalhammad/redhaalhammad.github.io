This is SSC Napoli One of the most historic football clubs in Italy.

Last year, during the 2022-23 Serie A season, SSC Napoli won their first league title since the 1980s and the third ever in the club's history. The team outperformed the expectations of fans and journalists alike with the help of a new manager, some new signings and a striker in excellent form - Victor Osimhen.

Unfortunately, this season is a much different story

After losing their manager and a key player from last season, Napoli has struggled to compete in the league this year. They are currently on their third manager of the season and have fallen to 9th on the league table, leaving both fans and players frustrated - especially Victor Osimhen who is entering the height of his career.

Hi I'm Victor

Now, it is all but guranteed that Victor - one of the club's all time best strikers - will leave Napoli at the end of the season. This ends his journey with the Southern Italian club in tremendously anticlimactic fashion. While he is largely responsible for winning them their first league title in 40 years, fans can't help but feel that the young striker is leaving behind what could have been a historic career in Naples.
